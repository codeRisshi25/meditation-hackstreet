About MediTree

Meditree is a website that offers meditation sessions with various sitting positions and accompanying music, incorporating a timer for convenience. Additionally, it features a unique partnership with NGOs to plant trees whenever users practice meditation on the platform, thus contributing to environmental causes.

Furthermore, Meditree provides a leaderboard feature that promotes a healthy competition among users, allowing them to track and compare their meditation progress. This comprehensive approach makes Meditree not only a tool for personal well-being but also a platform that fosters environmental sustainability and fosters a sense of community through healthy competition.
